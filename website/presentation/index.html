The problem

Daily life is fragmented
across a dozen apps and offices.

Africans juggle cash, mobile money, paper documents and queues at ministries — with no single source of truth for their financial or civic identity.

Five apps to pay a bill
Mobile money, bank apps, agents, cash — every transaction requires a different tool, and none of them talk to each other.
57% unbanked across region
02 · GOVERNMENT
Queues that cost a day
Renewing an ID, paying a tax, registering a marriage — all require an in-person visit, paper forms and hours of waiting.
